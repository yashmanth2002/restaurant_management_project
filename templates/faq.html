Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can browse the menu and add items to your cart. Once ready, proceed to checkout.

Can I order without creating an account?

Yes! We support guest checkout - just provide your contact details ordering.

How do I view my past orders?

Logged-in users can view their order history fromthe dashboard. Guests can use their phone number or customer ID

Is delivary available?

Yes, We offer delivery within select areas. Check your address during checkout.
